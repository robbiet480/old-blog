About

I am a 19 year old web developer and startup founder living in Oakland, California. You can follow me on Twitter, hear what I listen to on Last.fm or fork my code on GitHub. My e-mail address is me@robbiet.us.

This Site

I purchased robbie.io in 2011 and started it off with Tumblr. Today, it is automatically generated by Jekyll into a static site. Posts are in Markdown and the source resides on GitHub. Site is marked up with HTML5 and styled with CSS3. All pages should be valid and semantic. This theme is based on both Tom Preston-Werner and Tate Johnson's work with a little bit of the old Tumblr theme mixed it. If you find an error, please let me know.
